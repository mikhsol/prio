# Jeeves - Personal Assistant Mobile Application

## Product Brief

### Vision Statement
Jeeves is an AI-powered personal assistant that anticipates your needs, simplifies your daily tasks, and helps you achieve more while doing less. Unlike generic assistants, Jeeves learns your preferences, adapts to your lifestyle, and becomes your indispensable digital companion.

---

## Target Users

### Primary Persona: "Busy Professional"
- **Demographics**: 25-45 years old, urban, knowledge worker
- **Pain Points**:
  - Overwhelmed by daily tasks and decisions
  - Struggles to maintain work-life balance
  - Misses important events and follow-ups
  - Spends too much time on routine activities
- **Goals**:
  - Be more productive without burnout
  - Never miss important commitments
  - Have more time for what matters
  - Feel in control of their life

### Secondary Persona: "Organized Parent"
- **Demographics**: 30-50 years old, family-focused
- **Pain Points**:
  - Juggling family schedules
  - Managing household tasks
  - Coordinating with partner/family
- **Goals**:
  - Keep family organized
  - Reduce mental load
  - Have reliable reminders and scheduling

---

## Core Value Propositions

### 1. Proactive Intelligence
Jeeves doesn't just respond—it anticipates. It learns your patterns and proactively suggests actions, reminders, and optimizations.

### 2. Contextual Understanding
Jeeves understands context across your life—calendar, location, relationships, preferences—to provide relevant, personalized assistance.

### 3. Seamless Integration
Jeeves connects with your existing tools and services (calendar, email, notes, smart home) to be your single point of control.

### 4. Privacy-First Design
Your data stays yours. End-to-end encryption, on-device processing where possible, and transparent data practices.

## Core Features (MVP)

### 1. Smart Task Management
- Natural language task creation
- Intelligent scheduling based on priorities and availability
- Smart reminders with context-aware timing
- Task delegation and follow-up tracking

### 2. Calendar Intelligence
- Natural language event creation
- Conflict detection and resolution
- Travel time estimation
- Meeting preparation summaries

### 3. Daily Briefings
- Morning summary of the day ahead
- Weather and commute information
- Important reminders and deadlines
- Personalized news and updates

### 4. Quick Actions
- Send messages via voice
- Set timers and alarms
- Quick notes and voice memos
- Control connected devices

### 5. Conversational Interface
- Natural, context-aware conversations
- Multi-turn dialogue support
- Voice and text input
- Proactive suggestions

## Monetization Strategy

### Freemium Model

#### Free Tier
- Basic assistant features
- Up to 50 assistant queries/day

#### Premium ($9.99/month or $79.99/year)
- Unlimited assistant queries
- Advanced AI features (email, summaries)
- All integrations
- Multi-device sync
- Priority support

#### Premium+ ($19.99/month or $149.99/year)
- Everything in Premium
- Business integrations
- Advanced automation

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors
| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Differentiation |
|------------|-----------|------------|---------------------|
| Siri | Deep iOS integration | Limited intelligence | More capable AI, cross-platform |
| Google Assistant | Powerful AI, knowledge | Privacy concerns | Privacy-first, personalized |
| Alexa | Smart home, skills | Mobile-second | Mobile-first, personal context |
| Notion AI | Productivity focus | Not assistant-first | Conversational, proactive |

### Indirect Competitors
- Todoist, Things, Reminders (task management)
- Fantastical, Calendly (calendar)
- Otter.ai (transcription)
- IFTTT, Shortcuts (automation)

## Technical Requirements

### Performance
- App cold start: < 2 seconds
- Voice response: < 1 second
- Query processing: < 500ms p95
- Offline capability for core features

### Security
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Biometric authentication
- SOC 2 Type II compliance
- GDPR/CCPA compliant

### Accessibility
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
- Full VoiceOver/TalkBack support
- Dynamic Type support
- High contrast mode
