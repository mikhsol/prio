## Purpose

The Onboarding Flow introduces new users to Prio, establishes the privacy promise (critical for Maya), guides AI model setup, and captures their first task. A successful onboarding leads to first value within 60 seconds.

**Design Priority**: Maya needs explicit privacy reassurance. Alex wants to see value fast. Jordan needs goal motivation.
